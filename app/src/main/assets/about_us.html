Our Mission

Habit Tracker was created with a simple yet powerful mission: to help you build better habits while keeping your personal data completely private and secure.

Core Philosophy: Your habits, your data, your device. We believe that personal growth tools should be truly personal.

Why We Built This App

In an era where digital privacy is increasingly rare, we wanted to create a habit tracking app that:

  • Respects Your Privacy: Zero data collection, zero tracking, zero compromise
  • Works Offline: No internet required for core functionality
  • Focuses on Simplicity: Clean, intuitive design that gets out of your way
  • Promotes Real Change: Evidence-based habit formation techniques
  • Stays Accessible: Free core features with optional premium upgrades

The Science Behind Habits

Our app is built on proven scientific principles of habit formation:

The Habit Loop

  • Cue: Environmental triggers that initiate the behavior
  • Routine: The behavior itself
  • Reward: The benefit you gain from the behavior

Key Research Findings

  • 21-Day Myth: Real habit formation takes 18-254 days (average: 66 days)
  • Consistency Beats Perfection: Missing one day doesn't reset progress
  • Start Small: Tiny habits are more likely to stick than ambitious goals
  • Stack Habits: Linking new habits to existing routines increases success rates

Our Development Philosophy

Privacy by Design

Privacy isn't an afterthoughtâ€”it's built into every aspect of our app:

  • No user accounts or registration required
  • No analytics or tracking code
  • No advertising networks or third-party SDKs
  • All data encrypted and stored locally

User-Centric Design

  • Accessibility: Designed for users with disabilities

  • Customization: Adapt the app to your preferences
  • Performance: Fast, responsive, and battery-efficient

Technology Stack

For those interested in the technical details:

Frontend

  • UI Framework: Jetpack Compose (modern Android UI)
  • Architecture: MVVM with Repository pattern
  • Navigation: Compose Navigation
  • Animations: Compose animations and MotionLayout

Backend & Storage

  • Database: Room with SQLite
  • Encryption: SQLCipher for database encryption
  • Preferences: Encrypted SharedPreferences
  • Dependency Injection: Hilt

Security & Privacy

  • No Network Libraries: Zero networking dependencies
  • Local Encryption: AES-256 encryption for sensitive data
  • Secure Storage: Android Keystore integration
  • Code Obfuscation: ProGuard for release builds
