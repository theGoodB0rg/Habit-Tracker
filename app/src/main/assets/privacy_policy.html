Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 13, 2025

1. Privacy-First Design

Habit Tracker is designed with your privacy as the top priority. We believe that your personal data should remain personal and under your complete control.

Key Privacy Promise: All your data stays on your device. We never collect, transmit, or store your personal information on our servers.

2. Data Collection

Habit Tracker operates in a completely offline manner. Here's what this means:

Information We DO NOT Collect:

  • Personal identifying information (name, email, phone number)
  • Habit data or progress information
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Location data
  • Usage analytics or behavioral data
  • Crash reports or error logs
  • Any form of tracking cookies or pixels

Information Stored Locally:

  • Your habits and their details (name, description, icon)
  • Progress and streak information
  • App preferences and settings
  • Reminder configurations
  • Theme and customization choices

All this information is stored exclusively on your device using local storage mechanisms and is never transmitted anywhere.

3. Data Security

Since all data remains on your device, you maintain complete control over its security:

  • Local Encryption: Sensitive data is encrypted using Android's built-in security features
  • No Network Access: The app does not require internet permissions for core functionality
  • Secure Storage: Data is stored using Android's secure app-specific storage areas
  • No Third-Party Services: We don't integrate with analytics services, advertising networks, or cloud storage providers

4. Data Sharing

We do not share any data because we don't collect any data. Here's our commitment:

  • No data is transmitted to our servers
  • No data is shared with third parties
  • No data is sold or monetized
  • No advertising networks have access to your information

Export Feature: When you use the export feature to backup your habits, you control where this data goes (your device storage, email, cloud service of your choice).

5. Optional Features

Some features may require minimal permissions but maintain our privacy-first approach:

  • Notifications: Used only for local reminders, no data is transmitted
  • Email Feedback: If you choose to send feedback, this uses your device's email app with pre-filled device information for troubleshooting
  • App Store Ratings: Links to your app store, but we don't track this interaction

6. Children's Privacy

Since we don't collect any personal data, our app is safe for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect any information from children under 13 or any users for that matter.

7. Data Retention and Deletion

  • Retention: Data remains on your device until you choose to delete it
  • Deletion: You can delete individual habits, clear all data, or uninstall the app to remove all information
  • No Remote Deletion Needed: Since we don't store your data, there's nothing for us to delete on our end

8. Changes to This Policy

If we ever make changes to this privacy policy, we will:

  • Update the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy
  • Notify users through an in-app notice
  • Continue to maintain our privacy-first principles
  • Never retroactively collect data that wasn't collected before

9. Your Rights

Since all data stays on your device, you have complete control:

  • Access: Access all your data through the app interface
  • Modify: Edit or update any habit or setting
  • Export: Export your data in standard formats (JSON, CSV)
  • Delete: Remove individual habits or all data
  • Portability: Export and import your data as needed

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, you can contact us at:

Email: support@habittracker.app
Response Time: We typically respond within 48 hours

Please note that since we don't collect any data about you, we cannot identify you from any data on our end. Any communication will be handled through the email conversation itself.

11. Technical Implementation

For technical users interested in our privacy implementation:

  • Storage: Room database with SQLCipher encryption
  • Preferences: Android SharedPreferences with encryption
  • Network: No network permissions requested in release builds
  • Analytics: No analytics SDKs or tracking libraries included
  • Advertising: No advertising libraries or frameworks included

This privacy policy reflects our commitment to your privacy and data security. We believe that habit tracking should be personal, private, and completely under your control.
